Igual que eInfo() pero cambia los colores ( rojos )

WE._InfoColorBorder = "#E10000";
WE._InfoColorFormIni = "#FFf4f4";
WE._InfoColorFormEnd = "#ffdddd";
WE._InfoColorText = "#E10000";
WE._InfoColorShadow = "#B0B0B0";
